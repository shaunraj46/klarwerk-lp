Frequently asked questions

What exactly happens in the €50 consultation?

We map your top workflow(s), confirm volumes and exceptions, and estimate hours/€ saved. You get a 1–2 page "Automation Roadmap" PDF. The €50 is credited to your pilot.

How do you estimate savings?

We combine your volumes and manual minutes per item with a conservative automation factor (based on data structure and human-in-the-loop) and typical wage bands for DACH roles.

What does a pilot cost?

Most first pilots land between €2,000–€8,000 depending on inputs, integrations, and approvals. We agree a fixed scope before kickoff.

Will our data leave the EU?

By default we use EU hosting options. If a third-party requires non-EU processing, we flag it and offer an EU-only alternative.

Do you replace staff?

No. The goal is to remove repetitive load so your team can focus on higher-value work.
